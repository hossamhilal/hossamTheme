What Is Digital Marketing?

When it comes to digital marketing, it is a kind of advertising that focuses on your business promotion on the internet. Therefore, digital marketing is different from traditional marketing. Search engine optimization (SEO) is one of the most important forms of digital marketing. It consists of Push Digital Marketing and Pull Digital Marketing. The push digital marketing is done through email, RSS and SMS. It is a powerful way of attracting the targeted clients directly. On the other side, more clients can be grabbed by pull digital marketing. It compels the visitors to visit your site and make a call to action.
